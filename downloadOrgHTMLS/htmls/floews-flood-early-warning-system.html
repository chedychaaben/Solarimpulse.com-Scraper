About

"FLOEWS" is seeking to help solve the problem of ever persisting adversity of extreme weather event, natural disaster and climate-related risks such as flood which has accounted for about 71% of all death related to natural disaster & economic losses estimated in $Billion, by providing access to a democratized, socially inclusive and gender responsive disaster management and early warning intelligence to the vulnerable individuals, communities, cities, businesses & economies.
