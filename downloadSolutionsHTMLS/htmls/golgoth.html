Golgoth

A cleaner and more efficient cooling technology using water as a refrigerant

01.

Identification

Our solution fight Green House Gases (GHG) by eliminating the need to use Fluor-gases. We design and build game changing compressors and machines that produce clean cooling and clean heating using water (R718) as natural refrigerant through HVAC, Chillers and Heat Pumps. Our solutions are sober and use less electrical energy.
